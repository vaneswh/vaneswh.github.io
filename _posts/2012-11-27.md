很久之前，我在博客里记录过厦大每一栋楼都有特别的名字这件事，有的楼以捐赠人命名，有的楼以有寓意的语词命名。然后，一个博友在下面评论：海子的《面朝大海，春暖花开》里面，我最喜欢的是“给每一条河每一座山取一个温暖的名字”。

不知道为什么，我对这件事印象很深刻。

我觉得，起名字总是有原因的。有人会说，可能就是没有原因啊，不是什么事情都有原因的。我说，没有原因也是一种原因。（有人能明白我在说什么吗？）

今天想说的其实不是起名原因神马的，名字只是一个引子。

上午在病案科看医技科室工作量录入系统的时候，看到了A超这么个词，勾起了我的好奇心。原来还有A超，这我从没想到过。话说B超为什么叫B超呢？之前我也从未想过这个问题。

保持旺盛的好奇心太重要了。长大成人以后，我们开始对各种各样的事觉得理所当然起来，于是丧失了很多学习和进步的机会。

维基上的“医学超声检查”词条里给出的描述如下：

>医学影像学中常用的超声类型包括A型、B型、C型、M型、多普勒超声、脉冲反向谐波超声、谐波超声。
>1. A-mode: A-mode (amplitude mode) is the simplest type of ultrasound. A single transducer scans a line through the body with the echoes plotted on screen as a function of depth. Therapeutic ultrasound aimed at a specific tumor or calculus is also A-mode, to allow for pinpoint accurate focus of the destructive wave energy.
>2. B-mode or 2D mode: In B-mode (brightness mode) ultrasound, a linear array of transducers simultaneously scans a plane through the body that can be viewed as a two-dimensional image on screen. More commonly known as 2D mode now.
>3. C-mode: A C-mode image is formed in a plane normal to a B-mode image. A gate that selects data from a specific depth from an A-mode line is used; then the transducer is moved in the 2D plane to sample the entire region at this fixed depth. When the transducer traverses the area in a spiral, an area of 100 cm2 can be scanned in around 10 seconds.
>4. M-mode: In M-mode (motion mode) ultrasound, pulses are emitted in quick succession – each time, either an A-mode or B-mode image is taken. Over time, this is analogous to recording a video in ultrasound. As the organ boundaries that produce reflections move relative to the probe, this can be used to determine the velocity of specific organ structures.
>5. Doppler mode: This mode makes use of the Doppler effect in measuring and visualizing blood flow
>* Color Doppler: Velocity information is presented as a color coded overlay on top of a B-mode image
>* Continuous Doppler: Doppler information is sampled along a line through the body, and all velocities detected at each time point is presented (on a time line)
>* Pulsed wave (PW) Doppler: Doppler information is sampled from only a small sample volume (defined in 2D image), and presented on a timeline
>* Duplex: a common name for the simultaneous presentation of 2D and (usually) PW Doppler information. (Using modern ultrasound machines color Doppler is almost always also used, hence the alternative name Triplex.)
>6. Pulse inversion mode: In this mode two successive pulses with opposite sign are emitted and then subtracted from each other. This implies that any linearly responding constituent will disappear while gases with non-linear compressibility stands out.
>7. Harmonic mode: In this mode a deep penetrating fundamental frequency is emitted into the body and a harmonic overtone is detected. In this way depth penetration can be gained with improved lateral resolution.
