如题，一部被砍美剧（我都忘了是哪一年的新剧了），人人翻译为《不期而至》。不知怎的，第一眼看到剧名就想追了，是fall in love at first sight么（大雾

但是剧情决定了它只能被砍的命运。好在编剧在季终的时候给了一个可结可不结的ending，而我尤其喜欢Lexie做的优秀毕业生演讲，当时随手抄在了一张草稿纸上。

<blockquote>The laws of physics are basically the laws of life,
with all the unpredictable chaos around us,
there are certain universal constants,
gravity,the speed of light...
These constants never change,
even when everything else around them does.
Life is full of unknowns,
and when those unknowns are too overwhelming,
it's the constants that we have to hold on to.
Like our friends,the ones who are not afraid to tell us that there's no such things as normal.
The ones who have been in our life,for every minute with you,
even the hardest times.
Like those who could walked away,
but chose to stick around,
even though they had their own lives,families,their own children.
Like our parents,
because we couldn't be here without them...
who pick us up when we fall,
who come when we call them,
who answer when we knock.

We all learned what a light-year is,
and these years together,
have been our light-years.
The years where everything became brighter,
and we learned that the bright spots in our life aren't merely spots,
but constants.
And no matter where I go,
or what I do,
you are my constants.</blockquote>

这张废纸（上面有别的草稿和涂鸦）被我从家带到学校，几次收拾东西，几次想扔，却终究没舍得。

终于终于，我今天把它电子化了。

国庆回家之后，我好象厘清了一些想法，坚定了某种信念。

借用张佳玮在知乎上的某个回答作结语：<strong>一切都会好起来的</strong>，哪怕慢，哪怕曲折。只要你相信，只要时间过去。
